Decoupage is the art of decorating an object by gluing colour paper cutouts onto.
It can be decorated with special paint effects, gold leaf and so on....


We take what can seem like an ordinary shell and transform it into an item of even greater wonderment and beauty.

Nature places some stunning gifts on our local beaches. It is up to us to open our eyes to the treasures they can become.

We can personalise a shell with your own image or photograph. Just email us a copy in the best quality you can and our magic mermaids will get to work.
